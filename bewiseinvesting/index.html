Whether you are a novice or sophisticated investor, we believe that there is always something new to learn in the world of finance. When it comes to financial planning or planning a financial road map there are a number of things to consider .
  • Understanding interest rates and effects of compounding
  • Types of investments
  • Cost of investing
  • Risks in investments
  • Protecting your investments
  • Protecting your income from death and disability
  • Retirement
  • Transfer of assets to the next generation
  • Minimizing taxes on the estate
  • Paying lower taxes while you're earning
  • Tax strategies

Bewiseinvesting.com is your one-stop go to site for all of the above and more.
We understand the value that an advisor (Investment, Insurance, and Mortgage) brings to the table. With knowledge you're likely to have a more intelligent dialogue with your advisor. We want to help you build your knowledge in simple language and a better understanding of how investing in mutual funds, ETF's, GIC's work.
Need to understand better? Need to talk to an advisor of your choice? We will provide you with a list of qualified advisor in your area to help you with answers you might be seeking.
You could save thousands of dollars by getting the best and lowest rates for your mortgage. We will provide you with a list of mortgage brokers to talk to in your area.
Part of a financial plan is minimizing your taxes. Talk to a qualified accountant (especially if you are self-employed) to help you lower your taxes and have extra cash flow.
An important part of financial planning is a living Will and Power of Attorney. This is an integral part of estate planning or business succession planning. Need a lawyer in your area. We will provide you with a list of lawyers in your area specializing in this field to talk to.
