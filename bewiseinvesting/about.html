About Us

About Us

SPSly Pup was born out of shared frustration with the existing solutions in the industry. The existing software was dated, convoluted, unreliable and did not offer access to all the latest technologies. We found out that there were many others in the industry that shared the same feeling. We combined forces to crowd source on how to solve this problem by leveraging technology and making it simple. We challenged the status quo and started Sly Pup, using latest and reliable technologies at great price point and then threw in beautiful designs for free.

Our mission is simple, to create a software that adapts to your business needs. We are able to achieve this by leveraging our strength in technology and going direct to you, our customers, and engaging you to help us build a product you need. We trust that our customers will be our true spokesperson and partners in this journey.

Vision

Great products are built iteratively, continuously making improvements, keeping an eye out for new things and introducing new capabilities to meet customers need. We aim to be transparent with our product road map, listen to your feedback and promise to keep making Sly Pup better.
